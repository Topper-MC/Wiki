### `remain_time`

- **Format**:
  - <QueryCode p={props} type="global">&lt;holder&gt;;remain_time</QueryCode>
  - <QueryCode p={props} type="global">&lt;holder&gt;;remain_time;&lt;format&gt;</QueryCode>
- **Description**: The remaining time until the next reset.
- **Argument**:
  - `<holder>`: The name of the Holder
  - `<format>`: A time format pattern (e.g., `HH:mm:ss` for hours:minutes:seconds)
- **Example**:
  - <QueryCode p={props} type="global"><QueryVar client:load field="holder" />;remain_time</QueryCode>
    <ul>
      <li>Retrieve the remaining time in millis until the next reset of the Holder named <code><QueryVar client:load field="holder" /></code></li>
    </ul>
  - <QueryCode p={props} type="global"><QueryVar client:load field="holder" />;remain_time;HH:mm:ss</QueryCode>
    - Retrieve the remaining time until the next reset of the Holder named <code><QueryVar client:load field="holder" /></code>

### `end_time`

- **Format**:
  - <QueryCode p={props} type="global">&lt;holder&gt;;end_time</QueryCode>
  - <QueryCode p={props} type="global">&lt;holder&gt;;end_time;&lt;format&gt;</QueryCode>
- **Description**: The time of the next reset.
- **Argument**:
  - `<holder>`: The name of the Holder
  - `<format>`: A date-time format pattern (e.g., `yyyy-MM-dd HH:mm:ss` for year-month-day hours:minutes:seconds)
- **Example**:
  - <QueryCode p={props} type="global"><QueryVar client:load field="holder" />;end_time</QueryCode>
    - Retrieve the time of the next reset in epoch millis of the Holder named <code><QueryVar client:load field="holder" /></code>
  - <QueryCode p={props} type="global"><QueryVar client:load field="holder" />;end_time;yyyy-MM-dd HH:mm:ss</QueryCode>
    - Retrieve the time of the next reset of the Holder named <code><QueryVar client:load field="holder" /></code>
