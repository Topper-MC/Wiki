
### `top_name`

- **Format**:
  - <code>{props.globalPrefix || ''}&lt;holder&gt;;top_name;&lt;position&gt;{props.globalSuffix || ''}</code>
- **Description**: The name of the player at the specified position.
- **Argument**:
  - `<holder>`: The name of the Holder
  - `<position>`: The position of the player in the Holder
- **Example**:
  - <code>{props.globalPrefix || ''}{props.exampleHolder || 'money'};top_name;{props.examplePosition || '1'}{props.globalSuffix || ''}</code>
    - Retrieve the name of the player at position {props.examplePosition || '1'} in the Holder named <code>{props.exampleHolder || 'money'}</code>
